## Market Analysis Summary

### Controller System Distribution
- **PG Drives (R-Net/VR2/Pilot+)**: ~50% market share - Most common system
- **Dynamic Controls (LiNX/Shark/DX)**: ~35% market share - Growing rapidly  
- **Proprietary Systems (Q-Logic, VSI, Curtis)**: ~15% market share

### Leading Manufacturers by Revenue
1. **Pride Mobility** - $350M annual revenue, largest US market share
2. **Invacare Corporation** - $741.73M annual revenue (includes all DME products)
3. **Permobil AB** - $1.3B+ global revenue (premium segment leader)
4. **Sunrise Medical** - Major player with R-Net equipped chairs
5. **Golden Technologies** - Family-owned, US manufacturing, VA contract holder

### Key Connector Types for Adapter Design
For maximum market coverage, prioritize these interfaces:
- **R-Net DB9** (analog proportional) - Premium rehab market
- **VR2/Pilot+ 4-pin** - Entry/mid-range Pride models
- **Dynamic Shark 4-pin DCI** - Budget/import market
- **LiNX DX Bus** - Invacare and growing segment

**Together these cover approximately 85% of popular US wheelchair models.**
